# Wedvite: The Ultimate Cinematic Wedding Management Platform

Wedvite is more than just a digital invitation; it's a comprehensive, high-end wedding ecosystem designed to transform how couples invite, manage, and engage with their guests.

---

## 🌟 What is Our Product?
Wedvite is a **premium digital wedding concierge**. It combines stunning, cinematic web-based invitations with a powerful administrative dashboard that handles everything from RSVP tracking and dietary preferences to WhatsApp automation and guest analytics. It replaces traditional paper invites with an interactive, eco-friendly, and data-driven experience.

---

## 🎯 Who are we Targeting?
*   **Modern Couples**: Tech-savvy partners looking for a "wow factor" and a seamless way to manage their big day.
*   **Luxury & Premium Market**: Those who value aesthetics, smooth animations, and a curated guest experience.
*   **Destination Wedding Planners**: Experts who need to coordinate logistics (accommodation, transport) and track RSVPs across multiple events.
*   **Eco-conscious Hosts**: Couples wanting to reduce paper waste without compromising on elegance.

---

## 🚀 Why Choose Wedvite? (Our USPs)
1.  **Cinematic Experience**: Every invitation features premium "letter-by-letter" animations, background music, and interactive transitions that feel like a high-end film.
2.  **Granular RSVP Intelligence**: We don't just track "Yes/No." We track guest counts, dietary needs (Veg/Non-Veg/Jain), transport requirements, and event-specific attendance.
3.  **WhatsApp Integration 2.0**: Generate personalized invite links for different guest categories (Family, Friends, VIPs) with custom message templates.
4.  **Real-Time Analytics**: Monitor exactly when and where your invites are being opened, which events are most popular, and track guest engagement in real-time.
5.  **Logistics-Ready**: Built-in tracking for accommodation and transport needs, making it perfect for destination weddings.

## 🛠️ Feature Overview

### 🎨 1. The Guest Experience (Invitation Site)
*   **Hero Entrance**: Elegant, animated welcome screen with the couple's names.
*   **Countdown Timer**: Live countdown to the main event.
*   **Our Story**: A dedicated section for the couple's journey with rich media support.
*   **Event Timeline**: Interactive schedule of all wedding ceremonies (Haldi, Mehendi, Wedding, Reception).
*   **Venue Integration**: Embedded maps and one-click navigation to wedding venues.
*   **Interactive UI**: Smooth confetti effects, background music, and mobile-optimized responsive design.

### 📊 2. Admin Dashboard (The Nerve Center)
*   **Content Management**: Real-time updates to names, dates, stories, and images without needing a developer.
*   **Guest Management**: 
    *   Categorize guests (Family, Co-workers, VIPs).
    *   Bulk import/export guest lists.
    *   Assign guests to specific wedding "sides" (Bride/Groom).
*   **RSVP Dashboard**: Live feed of responses with automatic summaries of attendance and dietary needs.
*   **Personalization Engine**: Create custom greetings for different guest categories to make everyone feel special.

### 📱 3. Smart Communication
*   **WhatsApp Personalizer**: Generate hundreds of personalized WhatsApp links in seconds.
*   **Dynamic Templates**: Use placeholders to automatically include guest names and event details in messages.
*   **Category-Specific Links**: Send different messages or even different event access levels to different guest groups.

### 🏗️ 4. Advanced Architecture & Insights
*   **Real-Time Dynamic Updates**: Any changes made in the dashboard (photos, music, times) are instantly reflected on the live invitation site without needing a rebuild or redeploy.
*   **Tracking Engine**: Logs link clicks, device types, and engagement timestamps per guest.
*   **Success Metrics**: View heatmaps of which sections (Venue, Story, RSVP) guests are interacting with most.
*   **Export Reports**: Download guest data and RSVP summaries into Excel for printers or caterers.

### 🤝 5. Partner & Tier System
*   **Tiered Service**: Three distinct levels (**Classic**, **Premium**, **Grand**) to fit every wedding scale.
*   **Partner Portal**: Built-in support for Photographers and Event Planners to manage client weddings and track commissions.
